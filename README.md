# CreatorFi Artist Intelligence OS

A production-oriented monorepo scaffold for an **Artist Intelligence Operating System** that supports:

- artist scouting and qualification
- conversation tracking and follow-up
- lifecycle stage management
- opportunity/deal tracking
- contract and obligation workflows
- relationship health monitoring
- event-driven automations
- multi-agent assistance (Scout, Conversation, Lifecycle)

---

## Vision

Build a repeatable, data-driven system that moves artists from **first signal** to **long-term partnership** with clear decisioning at every stage.

---

## Lifecycle

1. Scout  
2. Qualify  
3. Contact  
4. Engage  
5. Negotiate  
6. Contract  
7. Activate  
8. Grow  
9. Retain

---

## Go-Level Decision Model

- **L1 Observe** — monitor only
- **L2 Test** — light outreach/pilot
- **L3 Build** — active opportunity
- **L4 Commit** — prioritize and fast-track
- **L5 Strategic** — long-term core partner
